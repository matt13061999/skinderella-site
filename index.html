Keira Hargraves
Owner of Skinderella

Hi, my name is Keira Hargraves, I was born at QMC and brought up in various parts of Stapleford. I did leave for a few years but couldn't resist coming back to my roots where people come together and help each other.

When I was young I attended Fairfield Primary School then moved onto Bramcote Hills Comprehensive then onto an apprenticeship to become a secretary which I did for a few years but wanted something a little different!

I began my piercing apprenticeship just over 15 years ago and after working at Bodycraft for 5 years I moved to mobile before starting at Treasured Ink but I soon outgrew there and decided to take the plunge to my own studio.

Today I am the owner of @Skinderella in Stapleford, where we do a big range of things. I have been married to the amazingly patient Sam for almost 6 years and we have 3 boys between us. Stapleford is our home.
